# Weekly Review Prompt

Run every Friday, ~5 minutes. Gets better every week as your brain grows.

---

## Prompt (paste into any AI tool with cerebellum MCP connected)

```
It's Friday. Please do a weekly review of my second brain (cerebellum).

Use the list_recent tool to pull everything I captured this week.
Then:

1. **Theme clusters** — group the thoughts by topic. What dominated my thinking?
2. **Unresolved actions** — find any action items that haven't been followed up on
3. **Interesting patterns** — what's recurring across multiple days?
4. **Connections I missed** — are there thoughts from this week that relate to each other in non-obvious ways?
5. **Gaps** — what important things happened this week that I probably didn't capture?

End with 3 specific things I should capture before next week's review to fill the gaps.
```

---

## What you'll get

A 5-minute synthesis that surfaces:
- The actual shape of your week (vs. your perception of it)
- Orphaned action items that would otherwise disappear
- Cross-domain connections only visible at weekly scale
- A capture checklist for next week

Becomes more powerful over time as the brain accumulates months of context.
