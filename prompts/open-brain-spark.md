# Open Brain Spark Prompt

Run this when you don't know what to put into your brain, or when you're staring
at the CLI wondering what to capture. It generates a personalized capture list
based on your specific work and life context.

---

## Prompt

```
I'm building a personal second brain called cerebellum — a database of thoughts
that my AI tools can query via semantic search.

I want you to interview me about my work and life to help me understand what
I should be capturing regularly. Ask me about:

1. The tools I use every day and what decisions I make in them
2. The people I interact with regularly (colleagues, clients, mentors)
3. The recurring problems I re-explain to AI tools or to myself
4. The projects I'm actively working on and their open questions
5. The knowledge I've built up that I keep needing to reference

After I answer, generate a personalized capture list organized by category
(People, Projects, Decisions, Patterns, References) with specific examples
of what I should be typing into cerebellum each day.
```

---

## What you'll get

A custom capture playbook that tells you exactly what kinds of thoughts are
most valuable to put in based on your real patterns — not generic advice.
